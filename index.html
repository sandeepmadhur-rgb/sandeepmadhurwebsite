How I Help

Most consultants start with dashboards and end with data paralysis.

I start with strategic clarity and end with actionable insights.

Planning

Set the Direction

You set the destination. We map the route.

Strategic thinking and cross-functional alignment. We determine where you're going and how you'll get there. This is your north star.

  • Rep capacity modeling (hiring, ramp, attrition, productivity)
  • Pipeline/demand planning across sources, segments, and products
  • AE success models (activity, opps, win rates, ASPs, sales cycles)
  • Quota and commission structures
  • Business models across SaaS, consumption-based, PLG, Partner-led, and HW & SW

Forecasting

Stay on Course

Traffic emerges, lanes close. We reroute in real-time.

Monthly/quarterly tracking against annual goals. A live, iterative process that adapts to changing dynamics - new competitors, market shifts, hiring delays, pipeline changes, product launches, etc.

  • Weekly/monthly analytical forecasting models
  • Cohort-based conversion tracking
  • Pipeline coverage and weighted pipeline ratios
  • Early warning signals for corrective action
  • Analytical forecast to complement rep & manager forecasting

Analytics

Optimize the Engine

Turn-by-turn guidance. Know exactly when to adjust.

Reporting informed by planning with the goal to improve forecasting & understanding of the business. Measure only what matters.

  • Sales efficiency deep dives (LTV/CAC, payback ratios)
  • Pricing and discounting curve analysis
  • Consumption pattern analysis
  • Rep-by-rep productivity analysis by cohort
  • Solving ambiguous business problems from first principles

About Me

I help Sales leaders at hyper-growth software companies scale their businesses more predictably through sales capacity planning & forecasting, pipeline generation planning, and pipeline conversion analytics.

My background bridges Finance and Revenue. I covered Enterprise Software companies as an equity research analyst at J.P. Morgan (#1 ranked by Institutional Investor in 2013), then moved to MuleSoft pre-IPO where I initially built out the FP&A function as the first Finance hire and built out the Sales Planning & Analytics function through the company's IPO and eventual acquisition by Salesforce.

Since 2019, I've been consulting with hyper-growth software companies, helping them navigate the transition from founder-led gut feel to data-informed decision making.
